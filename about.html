About Elevate IQ

Building tools people actually want to use

We started Elevate IQ because the tools teams were forced to use every day were slow, ugly, and got in the way of real work.

Elevate IQ is a small, fast-moving team building intelligent productivity tools for modern teams. We're remote-first, backed by strong investors, and obsessed with shipping things that genuinely make people's work easier — not just look nice in a demo. Today we're a team of 42 people across 12 countries, and we're growing fast.

Our mission

Make powerful tools feel effortless, for every team, regardless of size.

Our values

Ownership, honesty, and shipping over talking.

Our backers

Northbridge Capital, Forge Ventures, Atlas Partners.
