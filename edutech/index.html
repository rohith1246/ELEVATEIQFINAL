Who We Are

Built by hiring managers, not just teachers.

ElevateIQ started in 2019 when a group of engineering leads got tired of watching bootcamp graduates struggle in real interviews. So we rebuilt the curriculum backwards — starting from what companies actually screen for.

Mission

Close the gap between classroom learning and the job market with outcome-driven programs.

Vision

A world where your skills — not your degree or zip code — decide your career ceiling.

Core Values

Mentor-led, project-first, brutally honest feedback, and zero tolerance for filler content.

2019

Founded in a co-working space

Launched with a single 12-week Full Stack cohort of 24 students.

2021

First 1,000 placements

Crossed 1,000 alumni placed, with average time-to-offer under 45 days.

2023

210+ hiring partners onboarded

Formalized direct hiring pipelines with product, service, and startup partners.

2026

AI-assisted learning paths launched

Personalized roadmaps, resume analysis, and mock interviews powered by AI mentors.

Programs

The Path

One continuous thread from zero to offer letter.

This is the exact sequence every ElevateIQ student moves through — the line fills in as you scroll, just like your progress will.

01
Week 1–2

Beginner Foundations

Programming fundamentals, logic building, and the tools you'll live in — Git, terminal, and your first IDE setup.

02
Week 3–8

Core Programming Track

Deep dive into your chosen stack — Python, Java, or MERN — with weekly graded assignments and code reviews.

03
Week 9–13

Live Projects

Ship two production-style projects in small teams, using the exact workflow of a real engineering team.

04
Week 14–16

Internship

Apply your skills on a real client or partner engagement, with a dedicated engineering mentor reviewing your work.

05
Week 17

Mock Interviews

Structured technical and behavioral mock rounds with recorded feedback, run by our AI advisor and human mentors.

06
Week 18

Resume & Portfolio Building

1:1 resume audits, ATS optimization, and a portfolio site built from your actual capstone work.

07
Week 19+

Placement

Direct introductions to our 210+ hiring partners, with our placement team supporting you until you sign an offer.
