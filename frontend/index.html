Frequently Asked Questions

Have queries about our corporate structure, hiring tracks, or services? Here are some answers.

Is ElevateIQ an IT Consulting company or an Academy?

+

ElevateIQ operates as a dual-division corporate engine. Our core division specializes in IT and Non-IT business consulting solutions for global clients, while our EduTech Academy sub-division trains, certifies, and matches developers directly to our hiring teams and enterprise clients.

How can I apply to open career roles at ElevateIQ?

+

You can browse active professional vacancies directly in our Careers dashboard, or enroll in our specialized EduTech training programs. Graduates from these cohorts are automatically qualified for placement across our parent engineering teams and contracting clients.

Where is ElevateIQ Soft Tech located?

+

We are headquartered in Narasaraopet-Palnadu District,Andhra Pradesh, with remote consulting divisions operating across North America and APAC countries supporting diverse enterprise BPO and development contracts.
